Impact of technology in our daily life.

Technology has become more and more a part in our daily life,over the last 2 to 3 decades. we can see tech, gadgets, computers, and many other gadgets as well as tech appliances around us. All these technologies has transformed every aspect of our lives; some for the good and others may not be so good.

In this post, we are going to look how technologies have impacted our daily life.

Positive impact of technology in our daily life

Technology is everywhere- in almost every part of our life. it have affected, how we live, how we work and importantly how we learn.

It has played an vital role in making our lives easier. Technology helps us to keep in touch with people who are away from us. We use the telephones and computers to talk to them and even see them. Technology has made our life comfortable and also have become important part in our daily life.

Technology has good impact in education also. Technology has brought change in education, it is easier to get information about unknown topic or confused topic. Before the Internet and technologies, students used to visit libraries, spend time going and coming back, but now it is easy to type the name of a book in search engines and and read the whole book, etc. Today teachers use projectors as their teaching tools. Undoubtedly, technology plays very important role in education.
